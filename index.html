Luigi Bonati

I am a PhD student in the group of Prof. Michele Parrinello, ETH Zurich and USI Lugano. We develop new methods for studying the properties of condensed matter with the help of computational techniques.

I am currently focused on integrating machine learning and enhanced sampling to study rare events. Examples are the generation of machine learning based potentials for phase transitions and reactive events, as well as improving on enhanced sampling methods using deep learning and reinforcement learning schemes and the data-driven identification of collective variables.
