Chang Woo Myung

PostDoc @ETH Zürich & USI

I received my BS and MS at POSTECH and PhD at UNIST under the supervision of Prof. Kwang S. Kim. During my PhD, my main research focus had been on the first-principles calculation of energy materials (lead halide perovskite solar cells, electrocatalysts, and lithium ion battery).

Now, I joined Prof. Michele Parrinello group at ETH Zürich & USI, Lugano, Switzerland. My current projects are two-fold: (i) the effect of nuclear exchange in high-pressure hydrogen systems and (ii) development of graph-based machine learning molecular dynamics.

Please find my latest Curriculum vitae or my Google scholar page!

And check out my github repository for an implementation of Parrinello-Rahman barostat for path integral molecular dynamics on LAMMPS.
